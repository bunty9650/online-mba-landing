Is an Online MBA Degree Equivalent to a Regular MBA?

Yes. An online MBA from a university with UGC-DEB entitlement, NAAC accreditation, and required approvals (including AICTE where applicable) is considered equivalent to a regular MBA.

Can I get Online MBA admission without entrance exam?

Yes, selected universities offer admission without entrance exams. Eligibility depends on university rules and your qualification.

Can I study while working full-time?

Yes. Online MBA programs are designed with flexible learning, recorded lectures, and digital access for working professionals.

Which university is best for my budget?

It depends on your budget, profile, specialization, and preference for government or private university options.

Is counseling free?

Yes, counseling through onlinembaadmission.com is free. You can compare universities before making a decision.
